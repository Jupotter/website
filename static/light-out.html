Lights Out

Lights Out is a Seven Day Roguelike, where the gameplay is focused on managing you light sources.

Lights Out was a small game I developped back in 2012, with only seven days to work on it. Here it is as it was, with only a few modifications since.

Original Site

Explore the dungeon, armed only with your torches and your courage.

Enemies are attracted by light, but also injured by it. The stronger it is, the more life they lose.
Caution, your torches won't last forever. If the light goes out, well, you are likely to be eaten by a grue.
